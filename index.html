The Fischer Random Pawn Method

The Pawn Method is a simpler, faster way of setting up a starting position for Fischer Random Chess (aka Chess960). Unlike other methods, it doesn't require dice, coins, or a complicated process. Created by Robby Walker and Tim Suzman.


First-time Setup

On the bottom of each pawn, write the following:

♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟
QBBNNK/RK/RK/R
♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟
L1L3L5L7R1R3R5R7
