The Fischer Random Pawn Method

The Pawn Method is a simpler, faster way of setting up a Fischer Random (aka Chess960) starting position. Unlike other methods, it doesn't require dice, coins, or a complicated process.


First-time Setup

1. On the bottom of each pawn, write the following:

♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟
QBBNNK/RK/RK/R

♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟ ♟
L1L3L5L7R1R3R5R7
